Updated Blogging Workflow

Posted on September 30, 2024 by Loren

Hey friends, I’ve updated my process for publishing posts on lstep.xyz, and I’d like to share it with you. This streamlined approach helps me focus on writing while ensuring that my blog and its associated elements stay up-to-date.

The Workflow

  1. Writing the Post: I start by writing my posts in either Drafts or BBEdit, using Markdown format.

  2. Converting to HTML: Once the post is ready, I convert the Markdown to HTML.

  3. Integrating with the Blog Template: I then take the HTML content and add it to my new post template in BBEdit.

  4. Updating the Blog List: To keep my blog’s main page current, I update the blog list in BBEdit with information about the new post.

  5. Refreshing the RSS Feed: To ensure subscribers are notified, I update the rss.xml file in BBEdit with details of the new post.

  6. Publishing to GitHub Pages: With all the necessary files updated, I commit the changes to GitHub Pages. This step makes the new post live on my website.

  7. Cross-posting: Finally, I cross-post the content to Loren’s Blog, expanding the reach of my writing.

Conclusion

This updated workflow streamlines my blogging process, allowing me to efficiently create, publish, and share content while maintaining consistency across my blog. By following these steps, I can focus more on writing quality posts and less on the technical aspects of blog maintenance.
