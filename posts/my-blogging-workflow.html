My Blogging Workflow

Posted on August 31, 2024 by Loren

After reading Robertâ€™s post Our Blogging Workflow I decided to post mine. My blog has three post types: original musings, link posts, and link posts with replies. Here's how I create content:

Tools I use an iPad with Magic Keyboard and apps like Unread, Overcast, GoodLinks, Drafts, and 1Writer. My blog is hosted on Bearblog.dev.

Ideas I gather ideas from my reading (RSS feeds) and listening to podcasts. I save interesting content in GoodLinks, then export highlights to Drafts.

Writing I draft posts in Drafts, then proofread using Claude AI.

Publishing I copy the final markdown and paste it directly into Bearblog. I don't cross-post to other platforms.
