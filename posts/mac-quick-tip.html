Mac quick tip!

Posted on October 6, 2024 by Loren

Hey friends! Here’s a quick tip for Mac users. If you’re using Alfred and Moom, you can create a workflow that opens any app in the exact size and position you’ve set up in Moom. The magic happens with a simple AppleScript. I picked this up from Brett Terpstra the other day.

tell application "Moom" to arrange windows according to snapshot "BBEdit"

Now, whenever I launch BBEdit, it opens in the perfect spot and size on my screen, just the way I like it!
