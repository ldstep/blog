Preoccupied

Posted on September 28, 2024 by Loren

Hey friends, I haven’t written much this week because I’ve been involved in building my static website. I’m excited to say that it’s now up and running!

The process has been both challenging and enjoyable. Learning enough basic HTML and CSS to complete the project was the most demanding part. I watched several YouTube tutorials to get started, and found this one particularly helpful: Learn HTML – Beginner’s Course. ChatGPT was also a great resource for clarifying concepts I struggled to understand.

Another tricky aspect was setting up an RSS feed. With the help of some YouTube videos and ChatGPT, I managed to get one working.

Overall, it’s been a rewarding experience, and I learned a hell of a lot.
